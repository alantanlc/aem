Programmes  >  Programmes

To help raise the level of professional standards among the entrepreneurial community, the AEM offers programmes at non-graduate, graduate and post-graduate levels.

The Post-Graduate Diploma programme has been accepted as an MBA (Entrepreneurial Management) while the completion of the Certificate, Advanced Certificate and Diploma courses have been accepted as meeting the requirement for a graduate degree by some universities. The Certificate, Advanced Certificate and Diploma programmes add up to the fulfilment of a BBA in Entrepreneurial Management.
