Who is eligible?
Individuals occupied in business practice and students of entrepreneurial management are eligible for membership of the Academy of Entrepreneurial Management. Membership also allows members to place the membership abbreviation against their name as a mark of recognition.

Membership and Programmes Structure Relationship

Benefits
Membership supports entrepreneurs, budding entrepreneurs as well as executive and management staff of corporations for business development, with an extensive range of services to:

  1. Improve your entrepreneurial management skills and career prospects with our comprehensive range of qualifications.
  2. Develop your entrepreneurship potential or career through our Continuing Entrepreneurial Development Programs
  3. Develop and refresh your Entrepreneurial and Management skills through numerous training opportunities, development initiatives and on-line resources.
  4. Gain access to business opportunities through our on-line newsletter.
  5. Make valuable contacts and share ideas by networking with other entrepreneurs and managers.
  6. Gain access to exclusive member benefits on legal and financial services.
