Graduate Diploma in Professional Management (GDPM)

Overview
The Graduate Diploma in Professional Management is a three part programme spread over 3 years. Programme content includes evaluation of business concepts, development of sound business and marketing plans, legal responsibilities, financial management and competition. The study of entrepreneurship will provide information on generating innovative ideas, avoiding the problems and pitfalls of entrepreneurship, examining where and how to find assistance and support, applying learning to personal and business ventures. The programme is targeted at individuals seeking to equip themselves with the knowledge and skills to run a business. Those wishing to pursue business development positions in companies will also find this programme useful.

On completion of Part 1 of the course, students are awarded a Certificate in Professional Management. On successful completion of Part 2 in the second year, the student is awarded an Advanced Certificate in Professional Management and on completion of Part 3 in the third year, the student is awarded the Graduate Diploma in Professional Management.

Entry Requirements
Passes in five approved subjects in GCE examination or equivalent with a credit in English
or Advanced Diploma in Professional Management.

To enroll for the Advance Certificate in Professional Management, students are required to have successfully completed the Certificate programme. The minimum requirement for the Graduate Diploma in Entrepreneurial Management is the successful completion of the Advanced Certificate programme.

Course Methodology
The AEM courses are conducted by lecturers. Students are also given contact sessions with entrepreneurs to provide them real life examples and case-studies.

Examinations
Students are tested on course work and assignments. Post-Graduate Diploma students are required to complete a dissertation. Students are also expected to complete a business plan and/or conduct a business activity to meet the requirements of their qualification.
