Membership  >  Types of Membership
The Academy has developed different types of membership so you can choose the level that best reflects your achievements and helps you reach your personal goals. The membership application process allows for the correct level of recognition for individuals, appraising qualifications and experience as an entrepreneur, corporate intrapreneur or business development manager.

Student membership (M.A.E.M.(S))
Students are eligible to apply for the Student membership if you are studying, full or part time, towards a qualification in entrepreneurial management with the Academy. Our support and resources will make all the difference to you during your course.

Associate (A.A.E.M)
Students having successfully completed and passed the Diploma and have at least three experience in an executive or managerial capacity are eligible to apply for Associate membership. Those who completed the Diploma examinations are also eligible for associate status with a minimum of two years experience in an executive or managerial capacity.

Member (M.A.E.M)
Students having successfully completed and passed the Diploma and been enrolled as in Associate member with three years of executive or managerial experiences are eligible to apply for Member membership. Or has passed the Graduate Diploma Examinations and been enrolled as an Associate member and has held an executive or managerial position for a further two years following registration as an Associate member.

Fellow (F.A.E.M)
Membership is open for students who are over thirty years of age and has held an executive or managerial position for four years following registration as a member.

Honorary Fellow (Hon. F.A.E.M)
Honorary Fellow may be offered to any person who, in opinion of Membership Council has demonstrated outstanding entrepreneurial achievement. Their association with the institute ensures that their skills and experience are used to help to use the designator title F.A.E.M (Hon.) but are not required to pay any membership dues.

Professional Title - Certified Entrepreneurial Manager (C.E.M)
Students who has successfully completed the Post-Graduate Diploma and has contributed to the future of entrepreneurial management may be confers the Certified Entrepreneurial Manager.
