Admissions  >  How To Apply?

The Academy’s admission office accepts applications by post or email. Students are required to submit the following documents:

A) Application Form
Please print out a copy of our application form and fill in the required information. It takes about 15 to 20 minutes to complete the form.

B) Curriculum Vitae (CV)
Please submit a copy of your Curriculum Vitae (CV) along with your application form to the Academy of Entrepreneurial Management.

Please do not submit original certificates and documents. The Academy will not be held responsible for the damage or loss of original certificates or documents submitted. All documents submitted will not be returned.

Notification of Application Outcome
The applicant will be notified of his/her acceptance or rejection within 14 days of receipt of the enrolment documents in full. If the application is incomplete, missing documents will have to be submitted before the application is processed. Students are expected to be members of the Academy to enroll for the Academy’s examinable programmes.
