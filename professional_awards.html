Professional Awards  >  Professional Awards

To advance in today’s knowledge based economy (KBE), enterprises must have the ability to marshal knowledge, skills and creativity to improve their products & services for sustained growth and profits. Entrepreneurs would find, as time goes by, they would have to employ professionals to help them to manage more complex business chains in marketing, operations, finance, personnel and IT etc.

In this respect, when entrepreneurs require professional help to manage, say, investments, they will need to hire a professional in finance. However, no professional can do his best without clear instruction. To effectively manage these professions, the entrepreneurs must have the knowledge of and a degree of competence in, specific management function so as speak to the professions in ‘their language’.

It is to meet this need that the Academy introduced this professional management programs to equip aspiring entrepreneurs with knowledge and skills of functional managers so that they are not only proficient in key entrepreneurial management functions, but also able to manage their professional teams better, to advance their companies.

Programme Details
AEM Professional Management Awards (PMA) provides a framework for acquiring entrepreneurial skills and knowledge. The Awards have been developed specifically for the new economy, taking participants through transnational and cross-cultural contexts.

The Academy ensures that the standards embedded in the programme are globally recognized and valued by educational and organizations worldwide.

The course is designed to build on the knowledge gained whilst studying for the programme and also provides an ideal preparation for those planning to progress towards an MBA.

Assessment
Each core and major module is assessed by a set of assignments which is marked by the external examiners. Major modules give the candidate the opportunity to show their in-depth understating of the area of specialization.

Course Structure
The course is designed with five major components, i.e., Personnel, Marketing, Operations, Finance and Training. For each component, candidates are required to complete 4 core and two major modules.

Designed For
This course is designed for Founders, Presidents, CEOs and senior management of small to large sized companies.

Entry Requirements
Degree holders or graduates from a recognized university or professional body. Applicants without these qualifications but with extensive managerial experience may also be considered on a case-by-case basis.

Certification
Upon completion of the programme, candidates will receive a Professional Certification. For example, candidates who major in Operations Management will be awarded the ‘Certified Operations Officer’.

Certified Entrepreneurial Manager
This is the highest qualification of the Academy .It is awarded generally only for the candidates who have successfully achieved the Post-Graduate Diploma in Entrepreneurial Management with destination.
