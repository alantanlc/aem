Programmes  >  Programme Benefits

Individual
AEM’s Entrepreneurship training and education seeks to equip individuals with skills to sniff out business opportunities and utilize or command resources to earn profit from a business activity.

Individuals will find such skills useful in their own business or in setting up a business or helping their corporations develop new businesses.

A step-by-step approach, allowing students to assess their commitment and ability at each level, and exit with a recognized qualification at any level.

Companies
Corporation sending their staff for these programme will fund such staff have improved their business development potential and as intrapreneurs may help provide new opportunities for the corporation.

Government
Government officials either at local, state or federal levels will find officers with entrepreneurship to be more innovative in their utilization of resources for the good of the government and community. And for government agencies hamstrung by limited resources, the AEM courses would help staff manage and maximize use of their resources.

NGOs and Social Organizations
Non-Governmental Organizations (NGOs) and social organizations will find that officers trained in entrepreneurship may develop innovative revenue generating schemes as well as other means of ensuring their organizations’ goals are achieved.

Self-Sufficiency
Individuals as well as governments would find individuals completing the Academy’s programme more likely to depend on themselves creating employment opportunities rather than relying on others or the state to create jobs for them.
Such individuals would also be an asset in that they are more likely to create jobs for others thereby promoting business activity and income redistribution. Moreover, such training would help people move up the value chain thus helping communities move out of the poverty trap.
