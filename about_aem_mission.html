About AEM  >  Mission & Objectives

Mission
Our mission is to create economically self-sufficient individuals & communities through entrepreneurial development and education.

Objectives
The charter of the Academy includes:

  1. To promote and disseminate knowledge on entrepreneurial management.
  2. To develop educational programs that lead to the awards of professional qualifications in entrepreneurial and professional management.
  3. To publish papers and create a repository of knowledge in the field of entrepreneurial management.
  4. To promote business opportunities and networking sessions that would foster entrepreneurial development and to promote transnational business linkages and
  5. To work with national and international agencies to promote entrepreneurship and entrepreneurial management
