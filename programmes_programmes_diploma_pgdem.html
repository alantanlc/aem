Post Graduate Diploma in Professional Management (PGDPM)

Overview
The Post Graduate Diploma in Professional Management is a two part advanced programme in professional management to equip new entrants and working executives with more sophisticated knowledge of modern business practices. The Diploma would equip students with skills, analytical tools, perspectives, experiences and knowledge of managing a larger company. Those who wish to work for foreign companies or do business with foreign companies will find this programme particularly useful.

On completion of Part 1 of the course, students are awarded a Post Graduate Certificate in Professional Management. On successful completion of Part 2 in the second year, students are awarded the Post Graduate Diploma in Professional Management.

Students who pass their Post Graduate Diploma examinations with distinction would be granted the title - Certified Professional Manager (CEM).

Entry Requirements
To enroll for the Post Graduate Certificate in Professional Management, students are required to have successfully completed the Graduate Diploma programme or have completed a graduate programme from a recognized university. Similarly, to enroll for the Post Graduate Diploma in Professional Management, students are expected to have successfully completed the Post Graduate Certificate in Professional Management.

Course Methodology
The AEM courses are conducted by lecturers. Students are also given contact sessions with entrepreneurs to provide them real life examples and case-studies.

Examinations
Students are tested on course work and assignments. Post-Graduate Diploma students are required to complete a dissertation. Students are also expected to complete a business plan and/or conduct a business activity to meet the requirements of their qualification.

Exemptions
The Examination Committee may, at its discretion, allow exemption from some of the admissions requirements to mature students aged 25 years and above, possessing adequate level of education and practical business experience.
